This page lists some of the more common problems people have in-game.
Where can I get more help?
Click Here to view the online version of this page. The online version will be constantly updated with the latest and most annoying problems people have.
How do I take screenshots (without the HUD and weapon)
Pull out the camera (It's on the bottom of the pistol menu). If you press the left mouse button it will take screenshots. If you hold down the right mouse button you can zoom in and out. If you graphics card supports it you can turn on blur using the USE key (usually E).
How do I spawn stuff?
Hold Q down and press one of the buttons.
How do I pose ragdolls?
Use the physgun (it's at the bottom of the first menu). Grab a ragdoll by it's head (it should shoot out a blue beam). Now lift it up and press the right mouse button. It should lock the head in place.
How do I make the wheels go around?
With the wheel gun selected use the mousewheel to change the speed. You can also use + and - on the numpad.
My car goes around in circles!
One set of wheels are spinning the wrong way. With the wheel gun selected look at the wheel and press use (E) - this will reverse the direction of the wheel.
How do I rotate stuff?
With the physgun or the gravity gun hold down use (E) and move the mouse.
The flashlight is broken!?
No it isn't! It's meant to be like that!
The faceposing/paintgun doesn't work!
The faceposer and paintgun don't work properly online. Make sure you're clicking on "Start New Game".
